⬡ Run a Jet

A Jet is the router of the Safecloud network: it connects viewers to Drops, verifies micropayments, and settles them on-chain. Permissionless — no registration, no staking, no approval.

Setup — three steps, five minutes

1
Install and start (Node 18+, any VPS or home server with a reachable domain): 
git clone <your-qbix-app> && cd <app>
npm install
node server.js
2
Fund the printed address. On first start the Jet generates its own wallet, saves it to local/app.json (back that file up!), and prints its address. Send it ~0.01 BNB — that's transaction gas for settlements, roughly a month's worth.
3
Verify. Open https://your-host/Safecloud/health — you should see "signing": true. Your live dashboard is at /Safecloud/dashboard.

The economics

You earn a share of every chunk you route. Under the standard 90/10 policy the infrastructure share is ~10%, split between you and your Drops (default ~3% Jet / ~5% Drop / ~2% protocol — you set what you offer Drops, and their reliability adjusts it automatically).

Two revenue streams, same hardware: content delivery (viewers pay) and storage (owners pay — 100% to infra, no creator share). Storage is the base load; streaming is the upside.

Working capital: you pay your Drops with signed IOUs against your Safebux balance, replenished as viewer payments settle (automatic, every 15 min by default). Keep roughly one settlement-interval of Drop payouts on hand — the dashboard warns when you're low. Drops can verify your balance on-chain, so solvency is your reputation.

You can't cherry-pick lucrative content — chunks are encrypted and indistinguishable. Your only decision is price.

Configuration that matters

{ "Safecloud": {
  "requirePayment": true,
  "safebux":      { "address": "0x…", "perChunkWei": "500" },
  "openclaiming": { "address": "0x…" },
  "jet": {
    "minInfraBp": 500,          // reject policies paying infra < 5%
    "settleIntervalSec": 900,   // auto-settlement cadence
    "acceptedTokens": []        // additional {App}bux you accept
  }
} }

minInfraBp is your price floor — content offering less gets an automatic 402 with your terms. acceptedTokens makes token acceptance your market decision: accept an app's own currency if you trust its value.

Operating

📊
/Safecloud/dashboard — Drops, reliability, pending settlements, gas.
🩺
/Safecloud/health — JSON for your monitoring; alert on gasLow: true.
💧
Testnet faucet (optional, off by default): "faucet": { "enabled": true } lets new viewers get starter credits from your balance, rate-limited per IP.
FAQ

Can I be anonymous? Mostly. Your wallet is pseudonymous; your domain/IP is the visible part. Crypto-paid VPS + privacy domain gets close; viewers must be able to reach you, so full anonymity needs something like an onion service.

What if I go offline? Nothing breaks for anyone else — viewers find other Jets carrying the content. You just stop earning.

Do I hold user funds? Never. The payment rail holds nothing and you custody nothing — you route signed messages and settle them.
